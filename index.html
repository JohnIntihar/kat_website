About Me

I’m an abstract assemblage artist working out of Minneapolis. I focus on found and repurposed materials, and things typically considered junk. I’m inspired by texture, clutter, nature, and the “lived in” quality of old objects. I know someone’s my friend if they give me trash that made them think of me. Every piece is an experiment, and thus one of a kind. I can do commissions inspired by previous pieces, but never recreations.
